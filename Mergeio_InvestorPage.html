The Problem

The SME M&A market is fundamentally broken

28 million SMEs in the US. $2T+ in transactions every year. Yet the entire industry runs on phone calls and spreadsheets.

⏳

Too slow

Valuations take days or weeks. Buyers wait months for a credible number.

💸

Too expensive

A serious valuation costs $3,000–$5,000 minimum with a consultant.

🎲

No objectivity

No data-driven tool to compare acquisition targets side by side.

🔒

No marketplace

Deal flow is fragmented across brokers, directories, and word of mouth.

Our Solution

Everything a buyer or seller needs, in one platform

Mergeio brings AI-grade financial intelligence to a market that's never seen it before.

📊

AI P&L Analysis Engine

Upload any Excel P&L and get an instant health score (0–100) across 4 weighted financial criteria: revenue growth, EBITDA margin, cash flow consistency, and debt structure.

💰

Instant Valuation Range

Standard EBITDA multiple methodology, computed in seconds. The same approach used by M&A advisors — without the $5,000 fee.

🏪

Live Marketplace

Browse and filter acquisition targets by sector, health score, region, and asking price. Company cards with financial snapshot — no broker gatekeeping.

📈

Market Terminal

Real-time Bloomberg-style scoring dashboard. See the entire deal flow as it moves — which businesses are gaining score, which are declining.
